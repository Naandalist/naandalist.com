# Info Haji

App for Hajj candidates and families: check portion number, view departure estimation (Gregorian & Hijri), monitor queue position and quota, review payment status, and read curated Hajj news.

![Info Haji App Screenshot](@assets/images/thumbnail-infohaji-app.webp)

This application helps check portions by entering the portion number to view basic identity and queue details, complete with departure estimation projecting the year (Gregorian & Hijri) and queue position.

Users can monitor the latest provincial and embarkation quotas, review payment status including payment stages, reserve/istitha'ah status, and BPIH summary. The app also provides aggregation of the latest news from official and trusted sources related to Hajj, all with a privacy commitment without analytics SDKs and only calling official endpoints for queries.

## Users

- **Candidates & families**: monitor departure progress
- **KBIH/community mentors**: help verify pilgrim data
- **Volunteers/Hajj informants**: refer to quota data and official news

## Privacy & Compliance

- **Data collection**: does not collect personal data
- **Permissions**: minimum as needed for features; no broad storage access
- **Data sources**: endpoints and official information releases related to Hajj; source references displayed within the app
