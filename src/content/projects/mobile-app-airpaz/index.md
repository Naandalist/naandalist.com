# Airpaz: Flights & Hotels

Fly and stay anywhere with Airpaz, the all-in-one travel booking application for your vacation needs. Book cheap flights and hotels, enjoy exclusive travel deals, and manage your bookings anytime, anywhere.

![Airpaz App Screenshot](@assets/images/thumbnail-airpaz.webp)

## Highlights

New to Airpaz? Use promo codes for your region (e.g., **HAPPYHOLIDAY** for ID, **ADVENTURECALLS** for MY) to save on your first booking!

## Features

- **Book Flights Anywhere**: Search and book flights to popular destinations like Malaysia, Philippines, Thailand, Japan, and more. Choose from over 450+ airlines including AirAsia, Malaysia Airlines, and Singapore Airlines.
- **Hotels Worldwide**: Select from over 1.2 million properties globally. Filter by price, location, and amenities to find your perfect stay.
- **Flexible Payments**: Pay in your local currency with over 60+ options including credit cards, bank transfers, and e-wallets.
- **Easy Management**: Skip the queues with online check-in and manage your orders effortlessly.
