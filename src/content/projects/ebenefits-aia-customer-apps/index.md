# AIA eBenefits App

Designed exclusively for Singapore members of the AIA Employee Benefits Scheme, AIA eBenefits App makes it easy for you to access your insurance benefits any time, anywhere.

![AIA eBenefits App Screenshot](@assets/images/thumbnail-ebenefits-app.webp)

## Highlights

View your employee benefits at a glance, find nearby panel clinics with just a few taps and submit your claims from your smartphone.

## Features

- **Employee Benefits**: View your eCard and the employee insurance benefits available to you.
- **Panel Clinic Locator**: easily search and locate a panel doctor by location, district or medical specialty, with GPS integration.
- **Claims**: Submit claims seamlessly from your phone.

## Users

This application is designed for corporate employees in Singapore who are covered under the AIA Employee Benefits Scheme.
